Art Gallery

Our art exhibit, prepared by John Fernando, Braille Baril, Zoe Macarulay, and Maria Kathrina Montero, is inspired by artworks and creations in the Renaissance period. Otherwise known as the "rebirth" period of art, the renaissance period shifts away from religious forms of art to more historical works of art, not necessarily about religious figures like Jesus Chrst, God, or the Virigin Mary. In this exhibit, similar renaissance techniques such as realism, depth perception, light and shade, and emphasis on the human figure were also observed. The artworks in this exhbition take inspiration from the artworks of Da Vinci, Michelangelo, among others.
